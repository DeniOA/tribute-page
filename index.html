    Timeline of Tesla's life:

  • 1856-Borin in Serbia
  • 1861- Attended primary school in Smiljan
  • 1870- Moved to Karlovac to Gospic in the Austrian Empire.
  • 1882- Tesla moved to France where he began working for the Continental Edison Company, designing and making improvements to electrical equipment.
  • 1884- Tesla was hired by Thomas Edison to work at his Edison Machine Works.
  • 1885- Tesla said that he could redesign Edison's inefficient motor and generators, making an improvement in both service and economy. According to Tesla, Edison remarked,

    there's fifty thousand dollars in it for you-if you can do it


  • 1917- Tesla received AIEE's highest honor, the Edison Medal.
  • 1919- Tesla started working in Milwaukee for Allis-Chalmers.
  • 1928- Tesla received his last patent for a biplane capable of taking off vertically.
  • 1934- Tesla made claims concerning a "teleforce" weapon, after studying the Van de Graaff generator. Tesla had an accident in the fall of that year.
  • 1938- Early in the year Tesla was able to get up. He at once resumed the pigeon-feeding walks on a much more limited scale, but frequently had a messenger act for him.
  • 1943- On 7 Januray, at the age of 86, Tesla died alone and impoverished in room 3327 of the new Yorker Hotel.
